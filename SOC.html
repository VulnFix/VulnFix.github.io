 SOC as a service (SOCaaS)

Security Operation Center commonly referred to as SOC is a centralized location from where an organization’s entire IT environment is monitored to help prevent, detect and mitigate cyber attacks. 

Large enterprises have their dedicated SOC team to take care of the same. But mid sized and small sized companies due to insufficient staff and budget they leave security or SOC without doing. Some companies due to lack of awareness, they use to avoid these kinds of critical monitoring and altering systems.

After the advent of ransomwares, APTs and their pattern of target clearly shows that they are targeting more on small or mid sized companies due to security weakness and lack of awareness.
 
Vulfix comes up as a savior to these mid sized and small sized companies which acts as a SOC team on-behalf of the company and fights against the threats by providing SOC as a service.

Key Features:

  • 24/7 support for SOC events
  • Affordable prices
  • Highly qualified team
  • Experience in incident and threat handling
